Problem

DeFi liquidity is fragmented across chains and pools. Users waste time and fees on manual LP migrations. Protocols miss out on passive income from their own liquidity.

  • Manual, error-prone LP moves
  • Slippage & failed transactions
  • No protocol-level revenue

Solution & Value

Atomic Migration

One-click, fail-safe LP transfers between V2/V3 pools with strict slippage control.

Micro-Fee Revenue

5‚Äì10 bps captured at protocol layer. 100% flows to the Treasury multisig.

Partner SDK
