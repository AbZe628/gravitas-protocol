    Investment Thesis

    The maturation of DeFi requires a shift from retail-driven, high-risk yield farming to professional, risk-managed treasury operations. Currently, protocol treasuries, L2 foundations, and DAOs collectively manage billions in assets, yet lack the tooling to deploy this capital efficiently and safely. This creates a significant drag on capital efficiency and exposes ecosystems to systemic risks.

    The next wave of DeFi growth will be unlocked by infrastructure that provides operational trust and policy-based automation for on-chain capital management.

    Our thesis rests on three core assumptions:

    • Demand for Durability: Protocols will increasingly prioritize sustainable, long-term liquidity and yield over short-term, speculative APYs.
    • Operational Overhead is a Bottleneck: Manual treasury management is slow, error-prone, and does not scale.
    • Neutrality Wins: A neutral infrastructure provider that does not compete with its clients will become the trusted standard.

    Protocol Architecture

    Gravitas is a modular, multi-layered system designed for security, extensibility, and operational resilience. The core logic is separated from external integrations, allowing for rapid development and isolated risk.

    Core Components

    • PolicyEngine: The central smart contract that orchestrates all operations.
    • Adapters: A suite of modular contracts that translate PolicyEngine commands for external DeFi protocols.
    • Guardian & Timelock: Governance controls form the highest security layer.
    • Telemetry: On-chain events that provide a real-time, transparent data stream.

    Security & Assurance

    Our security posture is built on a defense-in-depth model, assuming adversarial conditions at every layer.

    Static & Dynamic Guards

    Static guards like reentrancy protection and adapter isolation are built-in. Dynamic guards include real-time circuit-breakers, oracle staleness checks (<60s), and volatility-aware slippage.

    Governance & Testing

    A 3/5 multisig with a 48h timelock governs protocol parameters. Our testing regimen includes Foundry invariants, fuzzing, >95% coverage, and mainnet-fork simulations.

    Monitoring & Events

    We monitor critical on-chain events: `FAILED_TX`, `CIRCUIT_BREAKER_TRIPPED`, `POLICY_BREACH_ATTEMPT`, `FEE_CHANGE_PROPOSED`.

    Investor F.A.Q.

    How do protocol fees accrue?

    Fees are programmatically swept into a dedicated multisig treasury controlled by the protocol. Initially, these funds will be used to cover operational expenses and fund further development.

    Is custody of funds ever taken?

    No. Gravitas is entirely non-custodial. Partner funds are managed via smart contract allowances and are always held in the partner's own contracts or in the underlying DeFi protocols.

    Why would a partner choose Gravitas over building in-house?

    Building and maintaining a secure, efficient, and audited liquidity management system is a significant undertaking that distracts from a protocol's core mission.

    What is the policy on a future token?

    A token is not required for the protocol to function or generate revenue. A token would only be introduced if it serves a clear purpose for decentralizing governance.
