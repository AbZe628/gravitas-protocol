A $100 Billion+ Monthly Problem

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) is hampered by a critical inefficiency: liquidity fragmentation. Capital is trapped in isolated pools, creating high costs for users and lost revenue for protocols.

The Market's Pain Points

  • Fragmented Capital: Liquidity is scattered across dozens of DEXs, reducing market depth and efficiency.
  • High Migration Costs: Moving liquidity positions involves multiple, complex transactions, incurring high gas fees and slippage.
  • Capital Inefficiency: Protocols cannot easily deploy their treasury liquidity to the most profitable venues, stunting growth.
  • Poor User Experience: Manual liquidity management is time-consuming and a significant barrier for both retail and institutional participants.

The Investment Thesis

  • Massive Market: DEX trading volumes consistently exceed $100 billion per month, a vast ocean of potential fee generation.
  • First-Mover Advantage: No existing solution offers true, atomic, cross-DEX liquidity migration. This is a blue-ocean opportunity.
  • B2B Focus: By providing infrastructure to other protocols, Gravitas scales with the entire DeFi ecosystem.
  • Essential Infrastructure: As DeFi matures, tools for capital efficiency are not just useful—they are essential.
