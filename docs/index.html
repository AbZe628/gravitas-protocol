Executive Overview

Problem → Solution → Value across creators, users, and the protocol.

Problem

DeFi liquidity lives in silos; manual moves burn time, slippage, and gas. Projects lack a predictable, recurring revenue stream from their own liquidity motion.

Solution

Atomic migrations across DEXs using audited adapters and strict guards (slippage, deadlines, whitelists), with transparent micro-fees that scale with volume.

Value

  • Creators: higher net yields, less operational drag.
  • Users: 1-click migrations, deterministic outcomes.
  • Protocol: usage-based, non-extractive revenue.

Market & Positioning

Agnostic migration infrastructure for a multi-venue, multi-chain reality.

Demand segments

  • DEX partners: seamless in/out flows; reduce user churn.
  • DAOs/Treasuries: programmatic treasury rebalancing.
  • MMs & funds: deterministic timing for rotations.

Differentiation

Neutral, audit-first transport with signature-stable APIs. No custody; deterministic execution with verifiable logs and rollbacks under timelock.

Threat Model (STRIDE)

Systemic view of plausible risks and concrete mitigations.

Spoofing

Signer spoofing, adapter address spoofing.

Mitigation: EIP-712 typed data; whitelisted adapters; on-chain registry.

Tampering

Parameter tampering in flight.

Mitigation: Timelock for admin changes; immutables; Merkle-proofed configs.

Repudiation

Dispute over who approved what.

Mitigation: Signed audit logs, append-only store, SIEM ingestion.

Information Disclosure

Leak of migration intents.

Mitigation: Commit-reveal for large moves; batching; optional MEV-protected relays.

Denial of Service

Griefing adapters, gas grief.

Mitigation: Circuit breakers; rate-limits per address; back-pressure queues.

Elevation of Privilege

Admin takeover, guardian abuse.

Mitigation: Multisig + hardware keys; scoped roles; emergency council with quorum.

Investor & Technical FAQ

How do fees accrue?
Micro-fees (bps) are deducted during migrations and routed to Treasury. Transparent, auditable accounting.
Is custody involved?
No. Gravitas orchestrates migrations via adapters with whitelisted routers; funds move through users’ authorized flows.
What’s the token plan?
Optional. Only after product-market fit: governance, fee-sharing, and staking bonds for security.
How are large moves protected?
Commit-reveal, batching windows, slippage caps, and optional MEV-protected relays.
