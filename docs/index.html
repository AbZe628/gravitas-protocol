    Investment Thesis

    The maturation of DeFi requires a shift from retail-driven, high-risk yield farming to professional, risk-managed treasury operations. Currently, protocol treasuries, L2 foundations, and DAOs collectively manage billions in assets, yet lack the tooling to deploy this capital efficiently and safely. This creates a significant drag on capital efficiency and exposes ecosystems to systemic risks.

    Our thesis is that a neutral, non-custodial infrastructure layer that provides operational trust and policy-based automation for on-chain capital management will unlock the next wave of DeFi growth and institutional adoption.

    Market, Model & Unit Economics

    The addressable market is the vast and growing pool of on-chain capital requiring professional management. Our business model is a simple, volume-based fee (5-10 bps), ensuring our incentives are aligned with our partners' success.

    Architecture Deep Dive

    Gravitas is a modular, multi-layered system designed for security and extensibility. The core logic is separated from external integrations, allowing for rapid development and isolated risk.

    Sequence of Operations

    1. Policy Validation: A transaction is initiated and verified against the treasury's registered policy constraints (allowlists, caps, etc.).
    2. Adapter Invocation: The `PolicyEngine` calls the appropriate, isolated `Adapter` contract to execute the transaction on the target DeFi protocol.
    3. Fee Sweep & Event Emission: A protocol fee is programmatically swept, and a detailed log is emitted as an on-chain event.

    Policy Constraints Framework

    • Asset Allowlist & Exposure Caps: Whitelists of permissible assets and maximum allocation percentages.
    • Oracle Freshness & Volatility-Aware Slippage: Reverts if price data is older than 60s and adjusts slippage based on market conditions.
    • Cooldowns & Velocity Limits: Time-based restrictions and limits on value moved per epoch.

    Security & Assurance

    Our security posture is built on a defense-in-depth model, assuming adversarial conditions at every layer. The protocol is designed to be failsafe, prioritizing the preservation of capital above all else.

    Static & Dynamic Guards

    Static guards like reentrancy protection and adapter isolation are built-in. Dynamic guards include real-time circuit-breakers, oracle staleness checks, and velocity caps to respond to market anomalies.

    Governance & Testing

    A 3/5 multisig with a 48h timelock governs protocol parameters, overseen by a pause-only Guardian. Our testing regimen includes Foundry-based fuzzing and invariant testing, >95% code coverage, and mainnet-fork simulations.

    Monitoring & Audit Plan

    We monitor for critical on-chain events (`FAILED_TX`, `CIRCUIT_BREAKER_TRIPPED`, `POLICY_BREACH_ATTEMPT`) and have a multi-stage audit plan including internal reviews, a top-tier external audit, and formal verification of core logic.
