Executive Overview

Problem → Solution → Value for creators, users, and the protocol.

Problem

DeFi liquidity sits in silos; manual moves waste time, create slippage, and add risk. Projects lack a predictable, recurring revenue stream from liquidity motion.

Solution

Atomic migrations with audited adapters and strict guards (slippage, deadlines, whitelists). Transparent micro-fees scale with volume.

Value

  • Creators: higher net yields, less ops drag
  • Users: 1-click moves, deterministic outcomes
  • Protocol: usage-based, non-extractive revenue

Market & Positioning

Agnostic migration infrastructure for a multi-venue, multi-chain reality.

Demand segments

  • DEX partners: seamless in/out flows; reduce user churn
  • DAOs/Treasuries: programmatic rebalancing
  • MMs & funds: deterministic rotations

Differentiation

Neutral, audit-first transport with signature-stable APIs. No custody; deterministic execution; timelocked rollbacks; verifiable logs.

Threat Model (STRIDE)

Systemic risks and mitigations.

Spoofing

Signer and adapter spoofing.

Mitigation: EIP-712, whitelists, on-chain registry.

Tampering

Parameter tampering in flight.

Mitigation: Timelock, immutables, Merkle-proofed configs.

Repudiation

Dispute over approvals.

Mitigation: Signed logs, append-only store, SIEM.

Info Disclosure

Leak of intents.

Mitigation: Commit-reveal, batching, MEV-protected relays.

DoS

Adapter griefing, gas grief.

Mitigation: Circuit breakers, rate-limits, back-pressure queues.

Privilege Escalation

Admin takeover.

Mitigation: Multisig + HW keys, scoped roles, emergency council quorum.

Investor & Technical FAQ

How do fees accrue?
Micro-fees (bps) are deducted during migrations and routed to Treasury. Transparent, auditable accounting.
Is custody involved?
No. Gravitas orchestrates via whitelisted adapters/routers; funds move through user-authorized flows.
What’s the token plan?
Optional after PMF: governance, fee-sharing, staking bonds for security.
How are large moves protected?
Commit-reveal windows, slippage caps, batching, optional MEV-protected relays.
