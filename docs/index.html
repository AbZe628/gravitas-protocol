Opportunity

Pain Points

  • Fragmented liquidity → shallow books & hidden costs.
  • Manual migrations → delays, errors, slippage.
  • No policy rails → zero repeatability or accountability.

Volumes keep growing; L2 fees enable frequent rebalancing. What’s missing is a **neutral transport layer** with an audit trail.

Investment Thesis

Over the next decade, **capital efficiency** wins. A neutral router that governs **flow** (not custody) creates a **composable moat**. We monetize **throughput**, not TVL.

  • Targets: DAO treasuries, L2s, large protocols, market makers.
  • Sticky integrations → adapters + policies + audits raise switching cost.

Architecture

Adapters

DEX Routing

  • Uniswap v2/v3 (exactIn/out)
  • Curve stables & metapools
  • Balancer weighted & boosted

Fail-fast, bounded external calls, safe math.

Lending

  • Aave v3 supply/withdraw

  • Canonical L2 bridges
  • External bridges (risk-scored)
  • Time-boxed quotas

Bridge risk = separate policies + hard limits.

Investor FAQ

Do you ever take custody?

No. Funds remain with the partner or the underlying protocols; Gravitas only orchestrates routes according to policy.

Why not build in-house?

Adapters + audits + ops + monitoring = a full product. We amortize cost/risk across many partners so your team stays focused on your core roadmap.

What’s the moat?

Integration network, operational trust and audit trail → high switching costs. Neutral infrastructure — we don’t compete with clients.

Token?

Not required for revenue. Considered **post-PMF** only for governance alignment.
